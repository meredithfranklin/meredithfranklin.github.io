Welcome.

About me: I'm an assistant professor of biostatistics at USC. My research specialization is spatial statistics applied to environmental data. I also teach courses in introductory statistics, inference, data analysis and spatial statistics.

Why I use git: I code in R almost exclusively, and while the statistical computing aspect of my research is primarily a solo endeavor, I like to keep my R code version controlled and in one place along with the Latex documents that eventually become presentations, papers or course materials.
